It works!

You are currently using theme-starter.css, a base template designed to show the available pieces with minimal styling. It includes album art, the song and artist, as well as other elements that may be useful in the context of streaming. To use a different theme, edit line 12 in public/index.html.

If you have knowledge of CSS, I highly encourage customizing this app to suit your needs.

Available Themes:

    A basic display of the available elements, built to be customized.
